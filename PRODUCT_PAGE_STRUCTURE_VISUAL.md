## Section Priority & Placement Strategy

### Above the Fold (Critical)

1. **Product Hero** - First impression
2. **Product Information** - Core purchase decision

### Primary Product Content (High Priority)

3. **Why You'll Love** - Product benefits
4. **The Art Behind** - Product story
5. **Specifications** - Product details

### Trust & Social Proof (Medium Priority)

6. **Testimonials** - Social proof
7. **Reviews** - Detailed feedback

### Brand Story (Supporting, Strategic Placement)

8. **Built by Artists** - Brand connection
9. **Our Promise** - Brand values

### Community & Cross-sell (Lower Priority)

10. **Join the Community** - Engagement
11. **Product Recommendations** - Cross-sell
12. **As Seen On** - Credibility

---

## Mobile Layout Considerations

### Mobile Stack Order

1. Product Hero (full width)
2. Product Information (stacked: image, then details)
3. Why You'll Love (2x2 grid)
4. The Art Behind (full width)
5. Specifications (list)
6. Testimonials (carousel)
7. Built by Artists (full width)
8. Our Promise (full width)
9. Reviews (full width)
10. Join the Community (full width)
11. Product Recommendations (2 columns)
12. As Seen On (3 columns)

---

## Content Hierarchy

### Visual Weight (from heaviest to lightest)

1. **Product Images** - Largest, most prominent
2. **Product Title & Price** - Clear, readable
3. **Add to Cart Button** - Prominent CTA
4. **Product Benefits** - Medium visual weight
5. **Brand Story** - Subtle, supporting
6. **Community/Cross-sell** - Lightest weight

### Information Hierarchy

1. **What it is** (Product)
2. **Why buy it** (Benefits)
3. **What makes it special** (Art story, exclusivity)
4. **Who made it** (Brand story)
5. **What others say** (Reviews)
6. **What else** (Recommendations)

---

## Key Design Principles

### Product-Centric Focus

- Product images and details are always most prominent
- Brand story supports but doesn't overshadow product
- Clear visual hierarchy guides eye to purchase

### Trust Building

- Social proof appears early (reviews badge)
- Trust badges visible throughout
- Brand story builds emotional connection

### Conversion Optimization

- Multiple CTAs (Add to Cart, Learn More, etc.)
- Clear value propositions
- Reduced friction (easy size selection, clear pricing)

### Brand Story Integration

- Subtle but present
- Enhances product value
- Creates emotional connection
- Doesn't distract from purchase flow
